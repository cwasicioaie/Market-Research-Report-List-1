<p><strong>The Multi Stage Beverage Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pump</li><li>Positive Displacement Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The multi-stage beverage pumps market consists primarily of two types: centrifugal pumps and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps use rotational force to move liquids, making them ideal for high flow rates and providing consistent pressure, suitable for transferring beverages like water, juices, and soft drinks. Positive displacement pumps, on the other hand, trap a fixed amount of liquid and force it through the discharge, offering precise flow control and higher pressure, often used in applications requiring consistent volume and viscosity management.</p></p>
