<p><ul><li>Closed Cell Rubber</li><li>Semi-closed Cell Rubber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EPDM foam rubber market is primarily divided into two types: closed cell rubber and semi-closed cell rubber. Closed cell rubber features a dense structure, preventing moisture intrusion and offering excellent thermal insulation and sound absorption, making it suitable for various applications, including automotive and construction. Semi-closed cell rubber has a mixed structure that allows some air flow while maintaining some insulation properties. This type is often used in applications requiring flexibility and increased cushioning, such as packaging and sealing solutions.</p></p>
