<p><p>The Transcatheter Mitral Valve Repair and Replacement market comprises two primary types: transcatheter repair techniques and transcatheter mitral valve replacement. Transcatheter repair techniques, such as edge-to-edge repair, involve minimally invasive procedures to enhance valve function without complete replacement. Conversely, transcatheter mitral valve replacement entails the implantation of a new valve via catheter, suitable for patients with severe mitral valve conditions. Both approaches aim to improve patient outcomes while reducing recovery time and surgical risks associated with traditional open-heart surgery.</p></p>
