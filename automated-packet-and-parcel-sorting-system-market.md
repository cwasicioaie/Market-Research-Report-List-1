<p><p>The automated packet and parcel sorting system market is primarily categorized into two types: linear sorters and loop sorters. Linear sorters operate on a straight conveyor path, efficiently sorting items based on size and destination using a single direction of movement. In contrast, loop sorters utilize a circular conveyor system that allows for multiple sorting paths, increasing throughput and reducing space requirements. Both systems enhance operational efficiency and scalability, catering to the growing demands of logistics and e-commerce industries.</p></p>
