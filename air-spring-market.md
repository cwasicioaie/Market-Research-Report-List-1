<p><strong>The Air Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Convoluted Bellows</li><li>Rolling Lobe Bellows</li><li>Sleeve Bellows</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air spring market features three primary types: convoluted bellows, rolling lobe bellows, and sleeve bellows. Convoluted bellows have a traditional design with a flexible, zigzag pattern that provides compactness and stability, ideal for light-duty applications. Rolling lobe bellows use a design where the bellows roll rather than fold, offering better load capacity and durability suited for heavy-duty vehicles. Sleeve bellows feature a cylindrical shape, providing excellent height adjustability and stability, commonly used in commercial and industrial systems.</p></p>
