<p><p>The ion implanters market is categorized into three main types: medium-current implanters, high-current implanters, and high-energy implanters. Medium-current implanters are designed for ion implantation at moderate currents, suitable for various semiconductor applications. High-current implanters operate at higher currents, allowing for faster throughput and are ideal for processes requiring high implantation rates. High-energy implanters provide ions with substantial energy, enabling deeper penetration into substrates, which is essential for specific doping requirements in advanced semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
