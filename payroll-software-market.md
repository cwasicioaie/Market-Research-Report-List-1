<p><p>The payroll software market is divided into two primary types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based payroll software is hosted online, allowing users to access it from anywhere with an internet connection, facilitating flexibility and scalability. In contrast, on-premises payroll software is installed locally on a company's servers, offering greater control over data security but requiring more maintenance and upfront investment. Both types cater to varying business needs, offering distinct advantages in terms of accessibility and management.</p></p>
