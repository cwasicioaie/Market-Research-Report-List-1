<p><ul><li>Single-acting Actuators</li><li>Double-acting Actuators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vertical pneumatic actuator market consists of two main types: single-acting and double-acting actuators. Single-acting actuators utilize air pressure to move in one direction and are typically equipped with a spring to return to the original position, making them ideal for applications requiring less force and simplicity. In contrast, double-acting actuators use air pressure to move in both directions, offering greater control and force, which makes them suitable for more demanding tasks in industrial automation and processes.</p></p>
