<p><ul><li>Infrared Induction Mode</li><li>Three Roll Brake System</li><li>Gravity Induction Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passenger Flow Statistics Systems encompass various technologies for monitoring and managing foot traffic in public spaces. The Infrared Induction Mode utilizes infrared sensors to detect persons entering or exiting areas, ensuring accurate counting. The Three Roll Brake System incorporates a mechanism with three rotating bars to control and track passenger movement effectively. Meanwhile, the Gravity Induction Mode relies on the natural weight and movement of individuals to register their passage, providing a cost-effective and low-maintenance solution for passenger flow analysis.</p></p>
