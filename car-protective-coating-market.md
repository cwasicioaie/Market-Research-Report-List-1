<p><p>The car protective coating market consists of various types, including priming coatings, medium coatings, surface coatings, and finishing coatings. Priming coatings serve as a foundational layer to enhance adhesion and prevent corrosion. Medium coatings offer enhanced durability and protection against scratches and environmental elements. Surface coatings focus on providing a smooth finish while enhancing aesthetics and hydrophobic properties. Finishing coatings add the final layer, delivering a glossy appearance and additional protection, ensuring overall longevity and visual appeal of the vehicle's exterior.</p></p>
