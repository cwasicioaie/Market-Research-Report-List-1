<p><strong>The Blue Hydrogen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Steam Methane Reforming</li><li>Auto Thermal Reforming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blue hydrogen market primarily consists of two types: Steam Methane Reforming (SMR) and Auto Thermal Reforming (ATR). SMR involves extracting hydrogen from natural gas while capturing and storing carbon emissions. In contrast, ATR combines natural gas with oxygen and steam to produce hydrogen more efficiently while also minimizing carbon output. Both methods aim to produce hydrogen in a more sustainable manner, serving as transitional solutions towards a low-carbon future by reducing greenhouse gas emissions during production.</p></p>
