<p><p>The aerial ropeway market consists of two primary types: detachable chairlifts and fixed grip chairlifts. Detachable chairlifts feature chairs that can disconnect from the cable at stations, allowing for faster loading and unloading, enhancing passenger comfort and reducing wait times. In contrast, fixed grip chairlifts maintain a constant connection to the cable, resulting in a simpler design, typically used in smaller ski areas or for shorter durations. Both systems serve to transport passengers in mountainous areas or urban environments efficiently.</p></p>
