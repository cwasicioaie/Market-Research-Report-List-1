<p><p>Secondhand fashion platforms operate primarily in two market types: B2C (Business to Consumer) and C2C (Consumer to Consumer). In B2C, brands or retailers sell pre-owned items directly to consumers, often ensuring a curated selection and quality assurance. Conversely, C2C platforms facilitate peer-to-peer sales, allowing individuals to buy and sell used clothing among themselves. This fosters community engagement and often results in lower prices and unique finds, enhancing the sustainable fashion movement by extending the lifecycle of garments.</p></p>
