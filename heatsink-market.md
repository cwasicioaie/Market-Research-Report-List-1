<p><ul><li>Active Heatsink</li><li>Passive Heatsink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heatsink market is divided into two main types: active heatsinks and passive heatsinks. Active heatsinks utilize fans or pumps to enhance airflow and improve heat dissipation, making them ideal for high-performance applications such as gaming and servers. Passive heatsinks, on the other hand, rely solely on natural convection and radiation without mechanical assistance, suitable for lower thermal management needs, like in compact devices and consumer electronics. Each type caters to specific cooling requirements based on performance and efficiency.</p></p>
