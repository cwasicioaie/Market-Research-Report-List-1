<p><p>The Acrylic Polymers Pressure Sensitive Adhesives market is segmented into three main types: Solvent Type, Emulsion Type, and Light Curing Type. Solvent Type adhesives use organic solvents to dissolve polymer components, providing strong adhesion and durability but raise environmental concerns. Emulsion Type adhesives are water-based, offering ease of application and lower environmental impact, making them popular in many industries. Light Curing Type adhesives utilize ultraviolet or visible light to rapidly cure, providing high-performance bonding for specialized applications such as electronics and medical devices.</p></p>
