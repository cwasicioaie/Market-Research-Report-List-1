<p><strong>The Metallurgical Blower Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Blower</li><li>Positive Displacement Blower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallurgical blower market primarily consists of two main types: centrifugal blowers and positive displacement blowers. Centrifugal blowers utilize high rotational speeds to create airflow through the centrifugal force, making them ideal for handling large volumes of air at lower pressures, commonly used in ventilation and cooling applications. In contrast, positive displacement blowers work by trapping a fixed volume of air and displacing it through the system, providing higher pressures, which is essential for processes like material handling in metallurgy.</p></p>
