<p><ul><li>Non-contact Type</li><li>Contact Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bubble level gauge market is segmented into two main types: non-contact and contact gauges. Non-contact type bubble level gauges use advanced technology, such as laser or ultrasonic sensors, to measure the level without physical contact, ensuring accuracy and reducing maintenance. In contrast, contact type bubble level gauges rely on a physical mechanism, often a bubble in a vial, to indicate level directly. Both types serve various industries, including construction and manufacturing, catering to different application requirements and precision needs.</p></p>
