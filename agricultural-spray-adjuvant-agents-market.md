<p><ul><li>Activator Adjuvants</li><li>Utility Adjuvants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Agricultural spray adjuvants are classified into two primary types: activator adjuvants and utility adjuvants. Activator adjuvants enhance the effectiveness of pesticides by improving their spreading, sticking, or penetration properties. They enable better absorption of active ingredients, leading to improved efficacy. Utility adjuvants, on the other hand, serve various supportive roles such as reducing surface tension, preventing evaporation, or stabilizing formulations. Together, these adjuvants optimize pesticide application, contributing to increased agricultural productivity and environmental safety.</p></p>
