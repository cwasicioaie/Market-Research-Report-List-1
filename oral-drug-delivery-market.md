<p><p>The Oral Drug Delivery Market features various controlled release systems tailored for effective medication delivery. </p><p>1. **Diffusion Controlled Release System** relies on drug permeability through a polymer matrix. </p><p>2. **Dissolution Controlled Release System** functions via the drug dissolving at a controlled rate. </p><p>3. **Ion Exchange Resins Controlled Release** utilizes resins to modulate drug release via ion interactions. </p><p>4. **Hydro-dynamically Balanced Drug Delivery System** maintains buoyancy in the gastrointestinal tract for sustained release. </p><p>5. **Osmotically Controlled Release System** utilizes osmotic pressure to regulate drug release. </p><p>6. **Dissolution & Diffusion Controlled Release System** combines both mechanisms for optimized delivery efficiency.</p></p>
