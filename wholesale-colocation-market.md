This trend supports the growth of wholesale colocation as companies seek to optimize their operations and ensure robust connectivity.</p><p>Additionally, advancements in technologies such as edge computing and artificial intelligence are pushing organizations to seek more space and power capabilities within data centers. Geographical diversification, especially in emerging markets, expands opportunities for wholesale providers.
