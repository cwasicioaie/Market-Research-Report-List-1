<p><p>The plastering mortars market comprises several types, including premixed mounting mortars, premixed rush coats, and premixed plasters. Premixed mounting mortars are designed for bonding plaster to substrates, ensuring strong adhesion. Premixed rush coats are thin layers applied quickly to prepare surfaces for final finishing, enhancing adherence and smoothness. Premixed plasters are comprehensive solutions that offer decorative and protective finishes, available in various textures and colors, simplifying the plastering process while ensuring uniform quality and performance.</p></p>
