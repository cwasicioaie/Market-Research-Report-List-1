<p><ul><li>Low Sensitivity</li><li>High Sensitivity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal motor protector market is categorized into low sensitivity and high sensitivity types, based on their reaction to temperature changes. Low sensitivity protectors activate at higher temperature thresholds, making them suitable for environments with gradual temperature variations. In contrast, high sensitivity protectors respond more quickly to temperature fluctuations, providing better protection in applications where rapid overheating can occur. Both types are essential for ensuring the safe operation of electric motors by preventing overheating and potential damage.</p></p>
