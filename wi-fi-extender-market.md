<p><strong>The Wi-Fi Extender Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wi-Fi Range Extender</li><li>Wireless AP Router</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Wi-Fi extender market includes two primary types: Wi-Fi range extenders and wireless access point (AP) routers. Wi-Fi range extenders amplify existing signals to broaden coverage in homes or offices, eliminating dead zones. In contrast, wireless AP routers create new Wi-Fi networks, allowing for multiple connections and improved performance in larger areas. Both types enhance connectivity, but while range extenders focus on extending existing networks, AP routers are designed to establish new, robust connections in various environments.</p></p>
