<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic proximity sensor market is divided into two primary types: analog and digital. Analog ultrasonic sensors provide continuous output and can detect varying distances, ideal for applications requiring precise distance measurements. In contrast, digital ultrasonic sensors deliver discrete signals, indicating whether an object is within a certain range or not, making them suitable for applications that need simple, binary responses. Both types are widely used in automation, robotics, and obstacle detection systems, enhancing efficiency and safety.</p></p>
