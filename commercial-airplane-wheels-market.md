<p><p>The commercial airplane wheels market is primarily divided into two types: main wheels and nose wheels. Main wheels, located beneath the fuselage, are crucial for supporting the aircraft's weight during takeoff, landing, and taxiing. They are designed for high load capacity and durability. Nose wheels, positioned at the front, provide steering control on the ground and support the aircraft's nose during operations. Both types are essential for safe and efficient aircraft operations, influencing performance and maintenance costs.</p></p>
