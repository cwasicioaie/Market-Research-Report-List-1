<p><p>The sterilizing UV-LED market is categorized into three main types based on wavelength: UV-A LED, UV-B LED, and UV-C LED. UV-A LEDs primarily assist in curing applications and surface sterilization, while UV-B LEDs are used for medical therapies and some disinfection purposes. UV-C LEDs are the most effective for germicidal applications, targeting bacteria, viruses, and molds by disrupting their DNA and RNA. Each type serves distinct functions, making them vital in diverse sterilization and disinfection industries.</p></p>
