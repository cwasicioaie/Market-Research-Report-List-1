<p><p>Fire hydrant systems are essential for urban firefighting, and they come in two primary types: wet barrel and dry barrel hydrants. Wet barrel hydrants maintain constant water flow and are used in warmer climates, where freezing is not an issue. Conversely, dry barrel hydrants are designed to prevent freezing in colder areas by keeping water below the frost line until activated. Both systems ensure efficient water access for emergency personnel, tailored to regional climatic conditions and safety requirements.</p></p>
