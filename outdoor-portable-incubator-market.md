<p><p>The Outdoor Portable Incubator market is divided into two main types: hard and soft incubators. Hard incubators are typically made from rigid materials, offering enhanced protection against environmental factors and providing better insulation for temperature control. They are suited for harsh outdoor conditions. In contrast, soft incubators are made from flexible, lightweight materials, making them easier to transport and set up. They are ideal for more controlled environments and are often used in field research or remote locations where portability is essential.</p></p>
