<p><p>The POM plastic gears market encompasses various types, including spur gears, helical gears, and worm gears, each serving distinct applications. Spur gears feature straight teeth, providing efficient power transmission in compact designs. Helical gears, with angled teeth, offer smoother operation and higher load capacity, making them ideal for high-speed applications. Worm gears consist of a worm and gear wheel, enabling significant speed reduction and torque multiplication, suitable for space-constrained environments. Together, these types cater to diverse industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
