<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Inkjet Printing Technologies Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scan Printing</li><li>Single-Pass Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inkjet printing technologies are categorized primarily into scan printing and single-pass printing. Scan printing involves a print head that moves back and forth across the paper, applying ink in a series of passes, making it suitable for high-resolution images and detailed graphics. In contrast, single-pass printing uses a fixed print head that applies ink across the width of the paper in one pass, enhancing speed and efficiency, making it ideal for high-volume printing applications.</p></p>
