<p><p>The LVDT (Linear Variable Differential Transformer) displacement transducer market includes two main types: AC-AC LVDTs and DC-DC LVDTs. AC-AC LVDTs operate using an alternating current to provide precise displacement measurements and are widely used in industrial and aerospace applications due to their robustness. In contrast, DC-DC LVDTs use direct current, often providing better signal processing capabilities and integration with digital systems, making them suitable for applications requiring high accuracy and low power consumption, such as biomedical devices and robotics.</p></p>
