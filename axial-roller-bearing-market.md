<p><p>The axial roller bearing market is primarily segmented into two types: ball bearings and roller bearings. Ball bearings consist of spherical balls that facilitate smooth movement, making them suitable for low-friction applications. In contrast, roller bearings use cylindrical rollers to distribute loads over a larger surface area, offering higher load-carrying capacity and stability. These types are essential in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, enhancing efficiency and performance in machinery and equipment.</p></p>
